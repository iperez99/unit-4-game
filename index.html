Crystal Collector Game!

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your game total score goes above the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

    5. Hint: Each time the game starts, the crystals will be assigned a new number!
